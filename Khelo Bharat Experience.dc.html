The starting point

Six National Gaps, Six Platform Answers

The concept work identified six systemic gaps in Indian sport. Each one maps to a direct, buildable platform response.

GAPSports data scattered across NSRS, Khelo India, KIRTI, state portals and paper files.
ANSWEROne identity + one aggregation layer — every record attaches to one verified ID.
GAPGovernment funding is hard to trace; files move invisibly.
ANSWERScheme workflows with live file tracking (eOffice) and money tracking (PFMS).
GAPGrassroots talent doesn't know which schemes exist or how to reach them.
ANSWERAn eligibility engine that tells each athlete "you qualify — apply here."
GAPCSR money can't find verified needs; needs can't find funders.
ANSWERA CSR exchange: verified need bank + matching + public impact tracking.
GAPRising athletes are invisible to media, sponsors and their own communities.
ANSWERA verified, consent-managed showcase: profiles, laurels, DD Sports content.
GAPGood data brings no daily value to the athlete — so nobody maintains it.
ANSWERA Benefits layer — card, jobs, marks. Value in the pocket keeps data alive.

The golden thread

One Athlete's Journey — Meena, 12, Vidarbha

The real test of a "single platform" is a single story. Meena never uploads a certificate twice, never proves herself twice, never travels to be "surveyed". Data flows up from where she lives; benefits flow down to where she is.

1 · Discovered at school

Her PT teacher runs the half-yearly screening games. Her 30m sprint lands in the top percentile for her age band — the app flags her automatically. Her teacher earns scout points for the verified upload.

M5 SCREENING GAMES · M6 SCOUT REWARDS
2 · Profiled once, forever

With her guardian's consent, an Athlete 360 profile is created on an NSRS ID. Her school has no track — so the platform links her to a usable facility 4 km away, with slots reserved for school hours.

M7 ATHLETE 360 · M10 FACILITY LINKAGE
3 · Tested and selected

She clears district screening games and a KIRTI assessment — every score lands on the same timeline. The eligibility engine has already confirmed her state scholarship; the DSO routes it in one tap.

M5 · M7 · M3 SCHEMES
4 · Supported daily

Her scholarship is paid straight onto her Silver Khelo Bharat Card, which also opens the SAI facility gate and gives kit discounts. A CSR partner funds her spikes and travel through the verified need bank.

M14 SPORTS CARD · M17 CSR EXCHANGE
5 · Competing, protected

She enters sanctioned events from her app; results update her profile, her AI score and the state index automatically. A registry-matched coach manages her load; an ankle niggle goes through the injury network, not ignored.

M8 EVENTS · M9 COACHES · M13 INJURY CARE
6 · Recognised in education

Her state medal generates a QR-verified certificate: bonus credit in her board assessment, attendance relaxation for camps, and a story on the public portal. Her teacher is now a Gold Scout.

M16 MARKS FOR SPORTS · M18 MEDIA & LAURELS
7 · Secured for life

At 19, she browses sports-quota vacancies with a verified Sports CV built automatically from her profile — a Railways job while she trains for nationals. Later, the coaching pathway awaits.

M15 JOBS FOR SPORTS · M9 CAREER TRANSITION
Data protection & trust

Privacy Is a Feature, Not a Page

DPDP Act by designPurpose-wise consent, data minimisation, retention limits — and a consent vault every user can open and change anytime.
Children firstVerifiable guardian consent before any minor's data is captured — screening games included. Public analytics show aggregates only.
Health data is sealedMedical, injury and sports-science records open only to authorised medical roles. Doping flags follow NADA permissions strictly.
Role-based access + auditEvery view and edit logged — who, when, what, from where — with quarterly access reviews.
Verification registryEvery fact shows its verifier. Unverified data is visibly marked and excluded from decisions.
Security baselineEncryption at rest and in transit, MeitY-empanelled hosting, periodic audits, and a rehearsed breach-response plan.
Roadmap

Four Phases, Strict Gates

Registries before workflows, workflows before benefits, benefits before AI. Each phase ends with a measurable gate that must pass before the next begins.

Phase 0 · Foundation MONTHS 0–3 NSRS identity integration · Org directory (M1) · Federation registry seed (M2) · Design system + DPDP framework · Data evaluation #1 (M20)
GATE: master registries live; first data MoU signed or declined with reasons.
Phase 1 · MVP MONTHS 3–9 Athlete 360 (M7) · Schemes & file tracking (M3) · Facility registry (M10) + grievance (M4) · School App pilot, 2 states (M5, M6) · Basic dashboards (M19)
GATE: 50,000 verified profiles; scheme cycle −30%; pilot screening >80% of schools.
Phase 2 · Benefits & Scale MONTHS 9–18 Sports Card launch (M14) · Jobs aggregator (M15) · Events (M8) + CSR exchange (M17) · Coaches (M9) + assets (M11) · National School App rollout
GATE: 1 lakh+ cards issued; 500+ jobs listed; all national events on platform.
Phase 3 · Intelligence MONTHS 18–30 AI talent engine full strength · State Excellence Index (M19) · Marks for Sports pilot (M16) · Academy accreditation (M12) · Injury network (M13) + open data
GATE: index published quarterly; marks pilot with ≥2 boards; AI shortlists adopted.
